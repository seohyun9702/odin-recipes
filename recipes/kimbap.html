kimbap

This is what kimbap should look like

Kimbap is a cool food. I recommend anyone to make it.

You need following ingredients:

  • rice
  • seaweed
  • ham
  • pickled radish
  • spinach
  • carrots
  • sesame oil
  • sesame seeds

Follow these steps:

  1. Put handful of cooked rice on top of the seaweed and spread it across.
  2. Add all ingredients except for sesame oil at the bottom of the seaweed.
  3. Roll the seaweed into a cylinder.
  4. Brush sesame oil on top of the kimbap.
  5. Cut it up and serve.

    6. Tip: For extra goodness, sprinkle on some sesame seeds at the end!
